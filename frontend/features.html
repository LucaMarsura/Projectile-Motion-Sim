Features

Everything the PMAD simulator can do.

01

Trajectory Projections

Observe the results of your trajectory through multiple dependent variables — distance, maximum height, time, final velocity, and impact angle — as well as visually through a comprehensive graph showing the exact trajectory of your projectile motion.

02

Variable Inferences

Using the iterative properties of the PMAD simulator, you are given the option to infer one launch parameter — initial velocity, initial height, launch angle, or ambient gravity — with the three other independent variables and one dependent variable.

03

Energy Analysis

Another output of any given simulation is a comprehensive, three-point energy analysis observing energy compositions at the initial, peak, and final states.

04

Uncertainty Propagations

The PMAD simulator also provides an uncertainty value for iterative solves, displaying the accuracy of the computed inference.
